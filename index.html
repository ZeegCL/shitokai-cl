Our Philosophy

Shitokai embraces the values of tradition, discipline, and self-improvement. Our teachings are rooted in the ancient ways of karate, passed down through generations of masters. Join us on a journey of personal growth and spiritual development.

Training Schedule

Classes are available for all levels, from beginner to advanced. We offer morning and evening sessions, with special programs for kids and seniors. Explore our training options and become part of our martial arts community.
